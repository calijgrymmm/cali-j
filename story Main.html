Sypnosis

‎

Follow Haruki, a regular office worker from Japan, as he gets swept into a world brimming with magic and wonder. Suddenly, he finds himself in the enchanting land of Eldoria, facing an urgent quest to save the empire from certain doom.

‎

Alongside two fearless princesses, Seraphina and Elara, and their loyal guard Garrick, Haruki sets out on a perilous journey. As they encounter challenges, Haruki discovers his own hidden magical talents, gradually honing his skills and mastering potent spells.

‎

Yet, it becomes clear that Haruki's arrival in this world is no accident. He's been summoned for a specific, crucial task: to confront a menacing and uncontrollable force of magic threatening to obliterate everything.

‎

Facing daunting odds, Haruki and his companions must find strength in unity, tapping into their deepest potential. With intense battles, heart-pounding action, and a cast of unforgettable characters, this epic adventure will keep you on the edge of your seat.

‎

Come join Haruki on his quest to rescue Eldoria and unlock the true potential of magic!
